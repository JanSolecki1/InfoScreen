FAQ

Here are the questions people ask most often!

What is a CPFW?

Regarded as the fifth global fashion week, Copenhagen Fashion Week welcomes leading international press, buyers, and industry leaders across their global network to four days of shows, installations, and events alongside the trade show CIFF.

How are we funded?

Copenhagen Fashion Week is a foundation funded by industry contributions and commercial partnerships with Strategic Partner Zalando as the main contributor.

When is a CPFW taking place?

Copenhagen Fashion Week unveils the AW24 show schedule that will take place in Copenhagen from 29 January – 2 February.

Who founded a prices for CPFW?

The Wessel & Vett Foundation sponsored the prizes, and the winner received a cash prize of 500.000 DKK. Furthermore, Stem will gain valuable advantages through a partnership between the Wessel & Vett Fashion Prize and Copenhagen Fashion Week.

Where applied for CPFW NEWTALENT?

CPHFW NEWTALENT is a talent support scheme for Nordic emerging brands with less than five years in business.
